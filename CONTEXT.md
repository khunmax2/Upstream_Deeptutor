# DeepTutor Learning

The language of mastery-based learning and the Learner Anima companion that
reflects a learner's verified progress.

## Language

**Knowledge Mastery Profile**:
A learner's current mastery distribution across the four Knowledge Types and
all of their Mastery Paths, measured by the share of available objectives that
satisfy the tutor's own mastery gate for each type.
_Avoid_: Element mastery, subject-element profile, learning-growth radar
